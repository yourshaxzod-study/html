HTML5 Article and Section Elements

Understanding semantic content organization

What is an Article Element?

The <article> element represents a complete, self-contained composition in a document. It should make sense on its own and be distributable independently.

Use cases: Blog posts, news articles, forum posts, product reviews, comments.

What is a Section Element?

The <section> element represents a thematic grouping of content. It groups related content together but is not necessarily independent.

Use cases: Chapters, tabbed content, different topics within an article.

Document Structure Best Practices

Nesting Rules

  • <article> can contain <section> elements
  • <section> can contain <article> elements
  • Both can be nested within each other as needed

When to Use Article

  • Content that makes sense independently
  • Content that could be syndicated or shared
  • Blog posts, news articles, comments
  • Forum posts, product reviews

When to Use Section

  • Thematic groupings of content
  • Chapters or parts of a document
  • Different topics within an article
  • Tabbed content areas
